Turn your invoices and receivables into collateral.

A new DeFi primitive for decentralized lending and tokenization receivables

- Get Funded -
  • Upload your Invoice / Purchase Order and the Approver address.
  • Approver approves your application.
  • Peers bid to fund you.
  • Accept or reject bids.
  • Pay back the peers you accepted their bids.
- Fund Someone -
  • Browse through people/organizations requesting for funds.
  • Find the person/organization you want to find.
  • Enter the amount you want fund them with and the interest rate you want.
  • Wait for them them to confirm.
- Approve -
  • Approves Funding Request.
